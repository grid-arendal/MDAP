Marine Debris Action Planner (MDAP) is a joint project between GRID-Arendal and SALT to develop a model predicting where litter is accumulating on coastlines. The project is funded by Innovation Norway. We are currently (2018) developing the model in Lofoten, but will be expanding the model to other pilot sites in Norway later this year.
